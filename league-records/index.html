Playoff Formats by Era
2022–2023 · Seasons 1–2
6-Team Bracket
Quarterfinals → Semifinals → Championship Finals. 2-week matchup rounds. Top 6 teams qualify. Consolation bracket determines MiLB draft order.
2024–Present · Seasons 3+
8-Team Bracket
Divisional Series → Conference Series → World Series. 2-week rounds. Plus a 5th–8th place bracket. Consolation winner earns the 1st MiLB draft pick.
